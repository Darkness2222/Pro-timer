FAQ

How do the presenter and admin stay in sync?

Pro Mode uses a real-time backend so both screens share a room. Admin adjusts the timer; the presenter display updates instantly.

Does it work on iPad or phones?

Yes. SyncCue is mobile-first and supports iPadOS and modern phones. Fullscreen auto-fits the digits.

Can I disable sound and haptics?

Absolutely. Each timer has a gear menu with toggles for sound and haptics.

How does Pro billing work?

We plan to offer monthly and yearly options via Stripe. Your access ties to your account email.
